The "One Fund" Model

We don't offer complex rebates or vouchers. We offer a Single Green Fund. We pay a competitive rate per ton directly to the school. How you use that revenue is entirely up to you.

FOR THE SCHOOL

Facility Upgrades

Use the bulk funds for new shade ports, tech equipment, or general maintenance.

FOR THE STAFF

Staff Welfare

Allocate a % to the staffroom fund to reward the ground staff managing the bags.

FOR STUDENTS

Class Prizes

Students can get rewarded for their recycling by getting tuck shop spending money from the fund.

How The League Works

01

We help you allocate storage

We help you allocate storage space for the recyclables collected by students and parents.

02

You Run The Challenge

Students and parents compete Class vs. Class to fill the bags with recyclables. We provide the "Launch Flyer" template to get parents excited.

03

We Collect & Pay

We run a scheduled "Milk Run" to collect filled bags. We weigh them digitally on-site, and transfer the lump sum to your school's green fund.
